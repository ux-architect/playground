    'Lucian Blaga' University Library

    From my thesis project in UTCN School of Architecure: Urban Restructuring in Sibiu - my home town
    Project: revive a large 11 ha, abandoned, industial site, adjacent to the historical center of the city. See location here

    The site is most valuable for its favorable position whithin the city and it is also bound by the Cibin River to the north. As one of the strategic, medium-term goals, for the city is developing its higher education sector, functionaly I opted for the site to become a university campus. Concentrating student activities here, would also boost the economic activities in the historic center: win-win.

    The campus feaures:

    • about 1500 student accomodations
    • some 200 acomodations for profs, phds and postdocs, situated by the river
    • campus center with student worksops, restaurants, shops, open-spaces for activities, a flexible, multi-purpose auditorium
    • generous green spaces, mostly surrounding the site, a walkway by the river
    • learning spaces for ULBS, a Research Center
    • center piece is The Library - as the meeting spot of academic life - another shot of the library here , interior here

    Check out the whole project (and other) on this page

    ArchiCAD © , 3ds Max © , Sketch up , V-Ray © , Photoshop ©

    Concept for standard prayment website

    This design is part of an internal effort to standardize company products. This would involve:


    • normalizing the integration method with clients and other 3rd parties
    • one single, device-optimised, maintainble website
    • a modular approach for product features - design custom libraries and frameworks for both backend and frontend
    • detaching the product from client branding
    • techinical documentation for internal use
    • provide documentation and interactive mockups for clients and 3rd parties for smoother comunication


    The concept product here is siphoning ideas like:

    • one design for all devices
    • adaptable, skinable, modular widget-like content
    • painlessly integration accros multiple clients (note that cube3 is fictional)
    • straight-forward UI/UX
    • stressing company iddentity and trade : "Payments powered by Mi-Pay"


    Based on these principles, check out this html mockup aimed at improving communication with clients.
